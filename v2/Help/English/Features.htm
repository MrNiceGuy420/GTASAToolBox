Features

Was dich an der GTA San Andreas ToolBox bestimmt zuerst mal interesieren wird ist, was sie alles kann.

Deshalb habe ich hier eine Liste der wichtigsten Sachen zusammengestellt.

 

  • Send defaultcheats to GTA San Andreas (By using virtual keys)
  • Management of the User Tracks
  • Viewing the pictures of GTA San Andreas*
  • Teleport to any place in GTA San Andreas
  • Edit savegames**
  • Backup and restore savegames
  • Edit** and spawn vehicles and weapons
  • Changing of any memory address in GTA San Andreas
  • Edit** and unpack IMG-archives
  • Web-GUI***
  • Recording of videos with sound**
  • Automatic updates

* The pictures taken with the camera of GTA San Andreas or the screenshotmodule of the GTA San Andreas ToolBox.

** Not build-in yet

*** Still contains bugs
